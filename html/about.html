About Me

Exploring the Intersection of Technology & Humanity

My journey into technology began with a simple curiosity: I wanted to understand why a virtual currency like Bitcoin held real-world value and how a decentralized world could function. This initial spark led me into the vast world of Web3, where I discovered that concepts from DeFi to smart contracts were the perfect intersection of my passions for computer science and finance. Today, I'm driven not just by the technology itself, but by the potential to help build a more equitable and transparent digital future.

As I began turning ideas into applications, I realized that functional code is only the beginning; the real challenge lies in creating experiences that are 'better to use'. This led me to Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), where my focus is on bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and everyday users. Whether it's refining an app's user flow or exploring how to build trust in AI, my goal is to make technology feel intuitive, accessible, and ultimately, more human.
